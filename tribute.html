L.A YEARKULI

A lady of honour, integrity and respect.

Early Years of Yarekuli

Yearkuli was born in South-eastern region of Hyfrydol,Ratisbon. She was the second of three children in the Unser family. She had her primary education in CEDEC International Group of Schools. She concluded her secondary education in Nun Danket secondary school whre she built a reputation of excellence in all she did.

Yearkuli's Career

Excellence breeds success. Yearkuli concluded her seconfdary education as the best student in the whole province of Eventide. She was awarded several scholarships including the most prominent college in the nation of Areostar- The University of Herrscher.

Yearkuli went ahead and obtained her bachelor degree in Biochemistry. She later got her PhD in Biochemistry and was awarded a promotion in the research institute of Areostar where the top scientists and innovators where. She exceled and thrived finding expression in all she did.

Yearkulis's Influence

By the end of her journey in the Research Institute of Areostar (RIA), Yearkuli had won 14 baccalaureattes and 12 Nobel prizes for outstanding innovations and weapons of warfare. She represented her nation in the World Abstract BioChemical War and won the gold medal whist leading a group of 10 to victory. Asides that her fame spread all over the world because of her daring leadership attributes. She performed massive developments in the nation of Areostar and formed alliance with neighbouring nations to defend the home turf which they own against the people of Salve.

Also known as The Param D'efit, she was a woman of honour and excellence. She died at the age of 87 years.

This is The Tribute of L.A Yearkuli "Param D'efit". A true model.
